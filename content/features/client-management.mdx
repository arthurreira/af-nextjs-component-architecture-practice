## Complete context for every customer account

Client Management keeps your team aligned around the same customer facts: who owes what, what changed recently, and what actions are pending.

### Key capabilities

- Centralized client profile and billing context
- History of invoices, payments, and outstanding balances
- Contact details and account notes in one view
- Faster handoff between operations and finance

### Why it matters

With client context in one place, teams spend less time searching and more time resolving issues quickly.
