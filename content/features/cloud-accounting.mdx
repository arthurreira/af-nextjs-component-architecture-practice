## One source of truth for your books

Cloud Accounting keeps your financial records centralized and continuously updated. Teams can work from anywhere without version conflicts.

### Key capabilities

- Always-on access from desktop and mobile browsers
- Shared visibility for finance, founders, and advisors
- Consistent data sync across invoices, clients, and reporting
- Reduced operational risk from local-file dependency

### Why it matters

When accounting data is always accessible, decision-making gets faster and month-end processes become less stressful.
