## Collaboration without compromising control

Multi-user Access lets teams and external partners collaborate while keeping visibility and permissions aligned with responsibility.

### Key capabilities

- Role-based access to limit sensitive operations
- Shared workflows for finance and operations teams
- Clear accountability for actions and approvals
- Controlled collaboration with accountants or consultants

### Why it matters

Teams scale faster when responsibilities are clear and access is intentionally scoped.
