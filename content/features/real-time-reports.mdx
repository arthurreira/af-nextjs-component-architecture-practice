## Insight without spreadsheet overhead

Real-time Reports turns raw transactions into readable financial views. Track performance continuously instead of waiting for periodic manual updates.

### Key capabilities

- Live dashboard views for revenue and financial health
- Instant summaries for profitability and billing activity
- Faster reporting cycles for internal and external stakeholders
- Clear visuals built for operators, not just accountants

### Why it matters

Faster insight enables faster action. Teams can detect issues early and adapt before they affect growth.
