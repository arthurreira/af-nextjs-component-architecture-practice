## Invoice workflows that stay out of your way

Smart Invoicing is designed for teams that need speed and consistency. Build branded invoices, send them instantly, and track status from one place.

### Key capabilities

- Ready-to-send invoice templates for common billing patterns
- Automatic payment reminders based on due dates
- Line-item clarity for services, discounts, and tax
- Delivery and open-state visibility for better follow-up

### Why it matters

Your finance workflow should not depend on manual chasing. Smart Invoicing shortens billing cycles and improves cash flow predictability.
